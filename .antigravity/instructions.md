# Antigravity Instructions Boilerplate

## Goal
Maintain the same architectural and delivery discipline used across Cursor/Codex.

## Working rules
- Respect clean architecture boundaries.
- Keep changes incremental and scoped to request.
- Prefer production-ready defaults.
- Surface architectural/security/performance risks proactively.
- Keep PRs small and objective.

## Testing
- Prioritize critical-flow tests.
- Avoid vanity coverage metrics.
- Ensure important user journeys are covered by E2E in dedicated suite.
